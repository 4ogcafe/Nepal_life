My country Nepal lies between two big countries India and China. It is agro based country. It is very small but rich in culture, tradition, natural gifts and beauties. Many people live in Nepal Most of them are farmers. They belong to different casts, sub-casts and religions. They also belong to different professions. Some are doctors, engineers, farmers and teachers. Over fifty percent of them are literate. They have been living like brothers and sisters. They are united. They have solidarity and living harmoniously together. They love and respect the nations better than they love themselves.
My country is rich in natural gift and beauties. There are many rivers, streams and lakes. There are many beautiful mountains to the northem side. The highest peak of the world Mt. Everest is in Nepal. We can see different types of forest. People have got many advantages form these natural resources. Many tourists from others country come here. They want to see and enjoy natural beauties.
Nepal is a country with diverse climates. So there are varieties of natural vegetations. There is popular saying in Nepal- “ Green forest; the wealth in Nepal” In Hindu religion it s said that one three is equivalent to ten sons. The forest is as important as air, water and land. Tress are adapted to the cold and snowy climates. The forest provides soft wood for paper and furniture industries. The jungle in not only the habitat of the wild animals and birds but also the back bone of the ecosystem. Forest is a very important natural resource of a country. It helps to control the pollutions. Very strong and effective steps have to be takes stop deforestation. We must discourage deforestation. More over people can understand the importance of forest if they are made educated and conscious trough tour travelling and awareness programs.
